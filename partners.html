Some notes and advice you may find helpful.

  1. First of all, getting good odds is critical to any betting strategy. The difference between the best and worst odds 0ver a year is enough to wipe out the profit of a highly successful professional, so don’t get excited in the heat of the moment and accept lower odds. It is much easier to make money by finding the best odds than by finding more winners just in case you were ever in doubt. Our partners can’t be guaranteed to always have the best odds in the marketplace, that is impossible, but on average they are the top of the league at any given time and anyone who slips form this position will be pointed out by ourselves and eventually removed from the list.
  2. If you have a particular style of betting or a particular type of even that you specialise in, then it is worth looking closely at the bookies who offer best odds and deals for your are of interest. For example, if you like each way bets at higher prices, there are bookmakers who often pay exceptional odds and 5 places etc. Choosing the right one could really improve your bottom line.
  3. Don’t just blindly accept statements like best odds guaranteed, we see this every day from bookies who can be 20% below the better bookies or the exchange. When you read the small-print you will find it is often just a con. If you don’t have time or patience to study this, then just trust our list and you won’t go too far wrong.
  4. Using an online bookmaker and especially one that is integrated with ourselves should eliminate any last risk of a bet not being paid because of ambiguity about how it was written. When bookmakers refuse to pay, one of the commonest reasons and one of the few that are legally acceptable, is when there is doubt about precisely what the punter was betting on. These interfaces and our integrated betting slips go to great lengths to ensure as far as we can that you have stated clearly what you are betting on and we don’t expect this problem to arise when using our bet slip or when using the online interfaces of our selected partners.
